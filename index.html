Garage Works Inc. Specializes in..

All garage door makes and models including Northwest Door, Wayne Dalton, Overhead Door Company, Clopay and Amarr Doors. We also repair all major brand garage door openers such as, Liftmaster, Genie, Overhead Door, Linear and Marantec. We proudly provide garage door services throughout all of Northern Utah, ranging from Ogden to Provo and beyond. Whether you need a new door installed, or opener repaired, Garage Works will give you the best service at a lower price than our competition. We have been proud to call ourselves your neighbor since 1993 and are locally owned and operated. Our roots are in Utah, not our branches, which means we care more about helping you than we do about making a quick buck. We're available 24/7 to help you out when you really need it. We strive to provide the best customer service and skills in one locally owned and operated company. We aren't owned by a corporation so you don't have to worry about being pressured to buy products you don't need. Garage Works offers flawless service, maintenance, and installation leaves you with lasting results. The Garage Works team is knowledgeable and committed to providing our neighbors with fast and reliable service.
